## OSINT in DFIR
Open Source Intelligence helps gather contextual information about:
- Threat actors  
- Victim organizations  
- Infrastructure indicators  
- Potential attack paths  

## Sources
- Public reports  
- Social media  
- Whois, DNS, certificates  
- Breach data  
- Malware repositories  

## Use Cases
- Attribution  
- Understanding attacker behavior  
- Gathering IOC correlations
