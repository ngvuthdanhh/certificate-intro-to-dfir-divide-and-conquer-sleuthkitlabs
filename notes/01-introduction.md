# 01 – Introduction to DFIR

## What is DFIR?
DFIR (Digital Forensics & Incident Response) is the discipline of:
- Identifying security incidents  
- Collecting and analyzing digital evidence  
- Containing and remediating threats  
- Documenting findings for reporting and legal use  

## Core Components
- **Forensics** — Evidence acquisition, preservation, and analysis  
- **Incident Response** — Detecting, containing, eradicating threats  

## Why DFIR Matters
- Supports legal investigations  
- Reduces incident impact  
- Provides post-incident learning  
- Restores system integrity
