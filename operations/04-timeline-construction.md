# 04 — Timeline Construction

## MACB Timeline Model
- Modified
- Accessed
- Changed
- Born (Created)

## Normalization Rules
- Convert timestamps to unified timezone.
- Remove duplicates.
- Reconcile conflicting timestamps.

## Anomaly Detection
- Gaps or bursts of activity.
- Suspicious timestamp clusters.
- Future-dated or backdated events.

## “Divide & Conquer” Timeline Method
1. Break timeline into segments.
2. Identify pivot events.
3. Trace backward to find cause.
4. Trace forward to determine impact.
5. Remove irrelevant periods.
